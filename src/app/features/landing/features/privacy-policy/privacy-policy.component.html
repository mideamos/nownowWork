Privacy Policy

PollinLink's commitment to your privacy

Introduction

Welcome to PollinLink! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data to ensure the best experience for our users:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, company details, and role (agronomist or pollinator).
  • Location Data: Field locations for task management and coordination.
  • Usage Data: Interactions within the app to improve functionality and user experience.
  • Uploaded Content: Task-related notes, images, and attachments provided by users.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Facilitate communication and task coordination between agronomists, pollinators and more.
  • Improve scheduling, field management, and operational efficiency.
  • Maintain security and prevent unauthorized access.
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.

Data Security & Protection

We implement strict security measures, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data.
  • Restricted access to company-specific information.
  • Compliance with industry standards for data protection.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

  • We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Data may be shared with service providers who assist in app functionality (e.g., cloud hosting).
  • Aggregated, non-identifiable data may be used for analytics and performance improvements.

User Control & Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt-out of certain data collection features.
  • Request a copy of the data we store about you.

Retention of Data

We retain your information as long as your account is active. If you choose to delete your account, we will remove your personal data, except where legally required to retain records.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app, and continued use signifies acceptance of the updated policy.
