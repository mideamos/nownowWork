Built for every role in the field

Agronomists

Agronomists serve as the hub of coordination in PollinLink. They assign and schedule tasks, communicate directly with pollinators, crop inspectors, roguing crews, spray plane operators, and researchers. From uploading field photos and tracking seeding progress to approving shelter placements and flagging field conditions, agronomists use the app to ensure everyone is aligned and operations move forward safely and efficiently.

Pollinators

Pollinators receive task requests and updates directly from agronomists, manage their own schedules, and scan shelters and trays for tracking. They can adjust bee placements based on field readiness, monitor weather and spraying alerts, and ensure bees are deployed safely and efficiently.

Crop Contractors

Crop inspectors can view schedules, field conditions, and notes provided by agronomists. They access maps showing shelter layouts and flagged zones, and can submit inspection results directly into the app, creating a shared record that helps agronomists track field compliance and performance.

Roguing Crews

The roguing team gets real-time updates from agronomists on which fields are active, safe, or require caution. Access point instructions, flagged hazards, and spraying timelines are visible in-app, helping crews plan efficient, low-risk field visits and stay in sync with broader pollination timelines.

Spray Plane Companies

PollinLink integrates seamlessly with spray plan scheduling software, automatically updating when personnel are scheduled in the field. This real-time sync helps operators avoid conflicts without needing manual input from agronomists. It reduces the risk of accidental exposure, cuts down on last-minute cancellations, and protects spray companies from liability concerns.

Researchers

Researchers can collect and track data related to bee health, pollination timing, and environmental conditions. PollinLink supports field-level data entry, exportable reports, and structured observations, enabling research into pollination performance, chemical exposure, and long-term sustainability—without accessing proprietary company data.

Chemical Safety

PollinLink is built with worker and pollinator safety at its core. Our chemical safety system alerts users of recent or upcoming spraying events, preventing accidental exposure to harmful pesticides. Through integration with spray plane software and real-time field status updates, we ensure that fields are only entered when safe—protecting people while helping companies stay compliant with chemical application standards.

Collaboration

PollinLink bridges the gap between different users by providing a centralized communication platform. With role-specific logins, field-specific messaging threads, and integrated approval workflows, the app fosters efficient, transparent coordination across all stakeholders. Whether you’re scheduling a bee placement or reviewing field conditions, everyone stays connected and informed.

Task Management

From bee placements to crop inspections, PollinLink makes it easy to assign, track, and complete every task in your processes. Our intuitive calendar and checklist tools provide clarity on what's scheduled, what's pending, and what needs attention. With task notifications, crew management, and automated reminders, you'll never miss a critical step again.

Mapping

Our interactive mapping tools take field visibility to the next level. View shelter placements, flag hazards, approve layouts, and track field-specific issues with precision. Integrated mapping with GPS data ensures that every decision is backed by location-aware context.

Data Security

PollinLink is designed to protect your most sensitive operational data. We use industry-standard encryption, role-based access control, secure authentication protocols, and strict privacy policies to ensure that only authorized users access your information. With full compliance and robust backend protections, your data remains secure, always.
